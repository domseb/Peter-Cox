Focus

I work with people of all ages from late-adolescence onwards, and offer both short and long-term approaches in modalities that include counselling, psychotherapy, and clinical supervision. I offer both in-person and online services. My focus is on working in depth to help people resolve underlying dysfunctional patterns of thought, emotion, and behaviour, and to help people in becoming the fullest and best version of themselves.

The surface manifestation of dysfunctional patterns vary greatly and include addictions, depression/dysphoria, anxiety, obsessive-compulsive tendencies, repetitive relationship difficulties, eating disorders, sexuality-based issues, identity confusion, and a general lack of meaning in life.
